Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HofTAPS

General Questions

What is HofTAPS?

HofTAPS (Hofstra Textbook Academic Peer Swap) is a platform designed exclusively for Hofstra University students to buy, sell, and exchange textbooks with fellow students. Our goal is to help students save money on textbooks by connecting directly with other students on campus.

Who can use HofTAPS?

HofTAPS is available to all current Hofstra University students with a valid Hofstra email address. You must register with your Hofstra email to create an account.

Is HofTAPS free to use?

Yes! HofTAPS is completely free to use. We do not charge any fees for listing books, making purchases, or any other service within the app.

Selling Books

How do I list a textbook for sale?

To list a book:

  1. Log in to your HofTAPS account
  2. Click on "Sell Your Book" in the menu
  3. Enter the ISBN of your textbook (found on the back cover or copyright page)
  4. Our system will automatically fill in basic book details from Google Books
  5. Add information about the condition, price, and any notes/highlights
  6. Upload clear photos of your book
  7. Submit your listing
How should I price my textbook?

We recommend pricing your textbook at 40-60% of the original retail price, depending on its condition. Check similar listings on HofTAPS to get an idea of the current market value. Setting a competitive price will help your book sell faster.

Can I edit or remove my listing?

Yes, you can edit or remove your listing at any time. Simply go to your account, select "My Listings", and choose the listing you want to modify or delete.

Buying Books

How do I search for books?

You can search for books by:

  • Title or author in the search bar
  • ISBN number
  • Subject category

Use the filters to narrow down results by price range, subject, and other criteria.

How do I contact a seller?

When you find a book you're interested in:

  1. Click on the listing to view full details
  2. Click "Contact Seller" or "Make Offer"
  3. You can either accept the listed price or propose a counter-offer
  4. The seller will receive a notification and can respond through our in-app messaging system
  5. All communication happens within the app to protect your privacy
How do payments work?

HofTAPS does not process payments directly. We recommend cash transactions when you meet in person to exchange the book. Always meet in a public, well-lit area on campus for safety. Some popular meeting spots include the student center, library, or campus dining locations.

Account & Security

How do I create an account?

To create an account:

  1. Click "Sign Up" on the home page
  2. Enter your Hofstra email address (@pride.hofstra.edu)
  3. Enter your h700 number
  4. Create a secure password
  5. Complete your profile with your name
  6. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. We only collect information necessary for the functioning of the platform. Your contact information is not shared directly with other users unless you choose to do so. All communications happen through our in-app messaging system first, giving you control over what personal information you share.

What if I have a problem with another user?

If you encounter any issues with another user, please use the "Report" button on their profile or in your conversation with them. Our moderators will review the situation and take appropriate action if necessary. For serious concerns, you can also contact us directly through the "Contact Support" option in the Help Center.
